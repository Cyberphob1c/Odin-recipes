Delectable Duck Ramen

A divine broth, atop it sits a golden goose slow cooked and simmered perfection

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon chili oil
  • ½ teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Preparation

No preparation required! Simply leave out the chicken to defrost prior to cooking!

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan combine broth and noodles. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat; stir to break up noodles. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil and ginger. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

291 Calories 10g Fat 42g Carbs 7g Protein

Allergy information

Please be aware that this dish contains ginger, chili and sesame
